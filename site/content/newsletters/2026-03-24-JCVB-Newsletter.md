5. **😎 Summer Camp - July 13-17** - JC Volleyball summer camp will run July 13-17, with two sessions each day. The first session will be a **Beginner Session from 12-3pm**, followed by an **Intermediate Session from 3:30-6:30pm**. The beginner session will be primarily for students entering grades 5-7, and will be introductory and teaching elementary level skills. The intermediate session will be targeted for students entering grades 7-9 who have some familiarity with volleyball. Both levels will start with working on the fundamentals and are great ways to get some extra experience!
