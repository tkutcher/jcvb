1. 🏐 **3-3 In Conference Play** - After a tough home matchup vs. a solid Gilman team, we bounced back to a 3-0 win over Friends on senior night where our seniors shined! Seniors Jack Grabowski, Matt Wilson, and Rob Gaines combined for 12 kills to pace our offense, and had extra support from Sam Butler & Jackson DeReamer. Jack Grabowski also had one of his best defensive games of the year, with 12 digs and a serve-receive rating of 2.0! Brendan Stewart stepped in as Libero, recording 8 digs on the match with great serve-receive, and was dominant serving with 2 aces.  Next up we have a conference matchup with St. Paul's Wednesday, then a challenging match at Loyola Friday.
