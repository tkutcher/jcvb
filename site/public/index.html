The Program

One program. One standard.

John Carroll Boys Volleyball is a program built on leadership, character, and relentless competition. Fielding both JV and Varsity squads in the MIAA, our Patriots develop players on and off the court — chasing championships while building the kind of culture that lasts. In 2025 both teams reached the MIAA championship, capping a season of 400 kills, 128 aces, and 66 blocks.
