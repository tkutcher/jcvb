Monday · JCVB Newsletter

July 21, 2025

  1. 👟 Offseason Workouts - This week we will hold our last 2 offseason workouts (and we will be back in the upper gym)!

    • Mon 7/21 ⬅️ Today
    • Wed 7/23

  2. 🩺 Physicals - The Athletics Department sent out a reminder to have physicals completed and uploaded in to Magnus prior to the start of tryouts. Please take care of having your physical completed and submitted - as you may not be allowed to participate in tryouts until it is completed.

  3. 🏐 First Day of Tryouts 8/6 - Reminder that the first day of tryouts is 8/6, and from then on out we will plan to be weekdays from 3:30-5PM. Note that next week is part of the MIAA "blackout period" where all player/coach contact is disallowed.

  4. 👕 Gear Store ­- The gear store is live! Please visit https://jcboysvolleyball2025.itemorder.com/shop/home/ to view the options and place orders. Note that the store will close Sunday, August 17th! Orders should arrive 2-3 weeks after that, putting us on track for gear to arrive before regular season conference matches start.

  5. 🎊 Welcome Reception 8/14 - Save the date for a volleyball welcome reception on Thursday August 14th from 6-7:30pm in the courtyard! This will be open to all players (both Varsity and JV) and their parents. Please use the following link to RSVP so that we can get a rough headcount for food: Welcome Reception RSVP Link. I will have this link live through the tryout week.

  6. 📆 Season Schedule - The schedule can be considered pretty-much finalized. The game schedule can be seen at the jcvb-2025-schedule Spreadsheet, as well as on my JCVB iCalendar. 

See you all soon!
