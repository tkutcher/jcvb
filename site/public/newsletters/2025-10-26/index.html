Sunday · JCVB Newsletter

October 26, 2025

  1. 🏐 Last Week of Regular Season - We head in to our last week of the regular season sitting at #2 in the conference. A win over either Spalding Monday or at Friends on Wednesday would secure the 2 seed and a home playoff game next Wednesday 11/5! We are playing some great volleyball and looking to keep the momentum rolling into the postseason.

  2. 🗓️ This Week

    1. Monday 10/27 - Home vs. Spalding - JV @ 4pm, Varsity ~5:15pm
    2. Tuesday 10/28 - Practice, 3:30pm, Upper Gym
    3. Wednesday 10/29 - Varsity @ Friends 4:30pm (depart JC 2:30pm)
    4. Thursday 10/30 - Practice 3:30pm, Upper Gym
    5. Friday 10/31 - 🎃 Halloween 4v4 Tournament
    6. Saturday 11/1 - Optional practice/workout, 9:30am

  3. 🎉 End-Of-Season Save-The-Date 11/14 - Save the date for 11/14 where we will hold our year-end program celebration. More details to come!

  4. 🏐 Season Schedule - The game schedule can be seen at the jcvb-2025-schedule Spreadsheet, on my JCVB iCalendar, and via TeamSnap - I have loaded the full practice schedule in to the iCalendar, and I will be keeping it up to date as things change.
