Sunday · JCVB Newsletter

September 7, 2025

Trying out a new way to distribute! Hopefully this email avoids your spam filters, be sure to add this email address to your safe senders just in case!

  1. 🚌 Bus Policy - Remember, if you are not taking the bus home from an away game then the coach who is in charge must have record (from a parent) that the student will not be taking the bus. This can be a TeamSnap message, an email, or a signature on the sign-out sheet. Either myself or Coach Tony will be in charge for each bus trip - let us know if you have any questions.

  2. 🗓️ This Week!

    1. Monday 9/8 - Practice in the Upper Gym 3:30pm
    2. Tuesday 9/9 - Away at St. Paul
    3. Wednesday 9/10 - Practice in Lower Gym 3:30pm
    4. Thursday 9/11 - Practice in Lower Gym 3:30pm
    5. Friday 9/12 - Practice in Upper Gym 3:30pm

  3. 📋 Looking for a Manager - I'm looking for one or two people who could assist in keeping the scorebook and serving as a manager for the season. This will go on a transcript and would help improve our gameday operations, plus lessen the burden of players to work the games. Players - please reach out to anyone who may be interested!

  4. 💉 Flu Shots - Flu Shot clinic at JC on Wednesday 9/10 from 5pm-7pm!

  5. 🏃‍♂️ Bailey's Heart and Soul 5K 9/14 - The track & field coach (Mr. Torres) passed along this info for a charity 5K at the school for a track athlete who passed away from a heart issue in 2021 https://www.baileysheartandsoul.org/baileys-heart--sole-5k-charity-runwalk.html. Some of the coaches will be running! Beat us and you can set the practice plan for Monday.

  6. 📆 Schedule Updates

    • Senior Night - 10/3 - Save the date for Senior Night on 10/3 (Friday night home game vs. Friends). Note that the game is scheduled for 4:30PM - so plan to be to the school for the recognition of our seniors well before game time!
    • Saint Paul's 9/9 & 10/8 Time Change - St. Paul's does not have a JV team this year. The 9/9 varsity game at St. Paul's will be 4:30pm, and the 10/8 home game will be at 4:00pm.

  7. 🏐 Season Schedule - The game schedule can be seen at the jcvb-2025-schedule Spreadsheet, on my JCVB iCalendar, and via TeamSnap - I have loaded the full practice schedule in to the iCalendar, and I will be keeping it up to date as things change. Currently, I am keeping JV & Varsity together in one calendar.
