Sunday · JCVB Newsletter

September 21, 2025

  1. 🏐 2-0 to Start MIAA - After dropping the first set in both JV & Varsity, both squads came back to sweep the next 2 & 3 sets to win their matches. Tyler Olivi's precision passing helped set up the offense, along with racking up a team-high 5 aces. Matt Wilson & Jackson DeReamer combined for 16 kills and 4 blocks helped win the matchup at the net.
