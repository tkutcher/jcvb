Sunday · JCVB Newsletter

July 6, 2025

Hello all – I wanted to send a brief summer update as we head into July and are a month away from our first day of tryouts!

  1. Communications Email List/Newsletter - For now, I will be communicating through this email list, newsletter-style. If there is any other email address to be added to the list, feel free to just email me here and I will make sure to include them. Similarly, if you want any email address taken off the list just let me know. Reach out to me at tkutcher@johncarroll.org.

  2. Offseason Workouts – We will be doing our optional offseason workouts Monday and Wednesday for the next 3 weeks, all running from 6-7:30pm. 

    • Mon 7/7
    • Wed 7/9
    • Mon 7/14 (lower gym)
    • Wed 7/16 (lower gym)
    • Mon 7/21
    • Wed 7/23

  3. Summer Camp Volunteering – If you are looking to volunteer for Coach Smith’s Summer Camp (beginners 7/14-7/18 4-6PM, intermediate 7/21-7/25 4-6PM), let me know and I can connect you with him.

  4. First Day of Tryouts 8/6 – Reminder that the first day of tryouts is 8/6, and from then on out we will plan to be weekdays from 3:30-5PM. 

  5. Current Schedule – Working game schedule can be seen at the jcvb-2025-schedule Spreadsheet, as well as on my JCVB iCalendar. 

  6. Gear Store ­– I’m working to get the gear store up in the next few weeks (presumably/hopefully in the next newsletter). I will keep you updated on that. 

That’s all for now – enjoy the rest of your summers and I hope to see many of you at the offseason workouts!
