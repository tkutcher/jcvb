Tuesday · JCVB Newsletter

November 4, 2025

Apologies for the additional email - wanted to send out some time corrections and provide the livestream links. Looking forward to some competitive volleyball the rest of the week!

  1. 🚨 GAME TIME CORRECTIONS - The last newsletter had some incorrect times, see correct information below:

    • Today's 11/4 JV Championship at BL begins at 4:00pm
    • Tomorrow's 11/5 Semi-Final vs. Curley begins at 5:00pm

  2. 🏆 JV Championship Today (11/4) @ Boy's Latin, 4:00pm

    • Our JV team will be playing in the conference championship today at Boy's Latin, 4:00pm! Varsity will be practicing back at JC then cheering them on via the Livestream Link below. Let's go Patriots!
    • 📺 Livestream Link

  3. 🏐 Varsity MIAA Semi-Finals Tomorrow (11/5) vs. Curley, 5:00pm

    • Varsity will be playing a big home semi-final matchup tomorrow (11/5) vs. Curley at 5:00pm. We hope many of you can make it and get the home support working to our advantage! If we win, we move on to the championship, which will be held Friday 11/7 at Mt. St. Joseph, 5:00pm, against the winner of Boy's Latin & Curley. Livestream link below.
    • ⬛️ BLACKOUT THEME - you are encouraged to wear your black JC attire to match the team's black uniforms!
    • 📺 Livestream Link

  4. 🎉 End-Of-Season Celebration - 11/14 - All players and their families are invited to join us on 11/14 for the end-of-season celebration where we will recap the season, announce some accolades, honor our seniors, and hopefully celebrate a successful postseason! Looking forward to see many of you there. Check the communications from Natalie Grabowski for RSVPs and other coordination. Thank you all for the support throughout the year!
