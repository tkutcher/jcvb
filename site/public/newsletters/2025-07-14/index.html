Monday · JCVB Newsletter

July 14, 2025

  1. 👟 Offseason Workouts - We are heading into our final two weeks of offseason workouts. A reminder, this week we are in the lower gym. To get there, when you go in the doors closest to the upper gym, take an immediate right towards the weight room and go down the stairs at the door left of the weight room entrance. At the bottom of the stairs, the lower gym is to the left.

    • Mon 7/14 (lower gym) ⬅️ Today
    • Wed 7/16 (lower gym)
    • Mon 7/21
    • Wed 7/23

  2. 👕 Gear Store ­– The gear store is live! Please visit https://jcboysvolleyball2025.itemorder.com/shop/home/ to view the options and place orders. As part of this I am also excited to show off some new branding for the program (images below!). Note that the store will close Sunday, August 17th! Orders should arrive 2-3 weeks after that, putting us on track for gear to arrive before regular season conference matches start.

  3. 🏐 First Day of Tryouts 8/6 - Reminder that the first day of tryouts is 8/6, and from then on out we will plan to be weekdays from 3:30-5PM.

  4. 🎊 Welcome Reception 8/14 - Save the date for a volleyball welcome reception on Thursday August 14th from 6-7:30pm in the courtyard! This will be open to all players (both Varsity and JV) and their parents. In the coming weeks, I will send out an email for RSVPs to get a rough headcount. We will have dinner and mini presentations to kick off the season.

  5. 📆 Current Schedule - Working game schedule can be seen at the jcvb-2025-schedule Spreadsheet, as well as on my JCVB iCalendar. 

Less than a month until we start! See you all soon.
